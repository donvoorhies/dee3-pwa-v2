About dee3:

dee3 (IRL: Don Voorhies) is – in the lack of better terms – a DK-based DJ.

An U.S. ex-pat, debuting behind the wheels of steel at the age of 14 during the tailgate of the disco-era in 1979, he continued on spinning on the mobile-scene and on-and-off in the club-scene in Jutland until 1986-87, when he started doing radio-work and spinning full-time as a club-DJ, until early 1991.

Later on, after gaining a Masters-degree in Information Science, he started in the early noughties working with - and trying out - a plethora of various computer-based DJ platforms that were then appearing on the market.

While trying out these various computer-based DJ platforms, he started compiling and mixing a mix-series called ”Compiling a Network” and later on another mix-series called ”dee3 | datamixes”; these two - now merged - mix-series' 24 sets have now seen way over 2 million downloads combined from listeners spread out from more than 190 countries acrosss the globe.
